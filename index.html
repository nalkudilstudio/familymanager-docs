Getting Started
Up & Running in 3 Steps
01

📲 Download & Sign In

Family Manager uses Google Sign-In for a secure, stable account that survives reinstalls.

  • Download Family Manager from the Google Play Store
  • Tap 'Sign in with Google' and select your account
  • Complete the quick onboarding — add your name and your partner's name
  • Your account is ready — no password to remember
  • Enable biometric lock in Settings for extra security
02

💳 Set Up Your Accounts & Opening Balances

Add your bank accounts, credit cards, and cash wallets so the dashboard reflects your real financial picture.

  • Go to Money → Accounts → Add Account
  • Add each account: Bank / Credit Card / Pay Later / Cash
  • Enter the current balance as the opening balance
  • For credit cards: add billing cycle day and payment due day
  • Transactions you log will update balances from this point forward
03

🔗 Pair with Your Partner

Family Manager creates a shared, synced space for two devices — yours and your partner's.

  • One partner opens Settings → Pairing → Generate Code
  • Share the 6-character code with your partner
  • Partner enters the code on their device and confirms
  • Both devices now sync transactions, occasions, accounts, and more
  • Each person's data appears correctly attributed to them

FAQ
Common Questions
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. All data is stored on Google Firebase (asia-south1 region), which is ISO 27001, GDPR, and SOC 1/2/3 certified. We use Google Sign-In for authentication — no passwords are stored by us. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
How does SMS auto-parsing work?
Family Manager reads your incoming bank and card SMS messages to extract transaction details. It uses pattern matching on the message text — no message content is uploaded or stored. The SMS permission is used only on your device.
Can I use Family Manager without pairing a partner?
Yes — all modules work for a single user. Pairing simply enables real-time sync of transactions, accounts, and family data between two devices. You can pair anytime after initial setup.
What happens if I reinstall the app?
Because Family Manager uses Google Sign-In, your account UID is stable across reinstalls. Sign in again with the same Google account and your data will be restored from Firebase automatically.
How does credit card balance work?
When you log a credit card transaction, your bank balance is not affected — only the card's outstanding balance increases. When you pay the credit card bill (Pay CC Bill), both the bank balance decreases and the outstanding balance decreases. This mirrors real-world deferred billing.
Can both partners add transactions simultaneously?
Yes. Each device pushes changes to Firebase independently. Transactions are attributed using SELF/SPOUSE roles that are translated correctly on each device — so Vidhya's transactions always appear under Vidhya, and Sarav's under Sarav.
