Privacy Policy

Section 01

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account details — name and email via Google Sign-In
  • Financial data — transactions, account balances, credit card details, loan records, recurring items
  • Family data — occasions, appointments, school records, grocery lists, home planner tasks, insurance, contacts
  • Family structure — member names and roles (SELF / SPOUSE)
  • Pairing data — a unique family ID generated when two devices are linked

Collected automatically:

  • Android version and device model for Firebase crash reporting only
  • No advertising IDs, no location data, no microphone or camera access
Section 02

SMS Permission

Family Manager requests SMS permission for the SMS Auto-Parse feature, which reads incoming bank and card messages to automatically extract transaction details.

  • SMS reading happens entirely on your device
  • No SMS content is uploaded to our servers
  • Only extracted transaction data (amount, merchant, date) is stored in Firebase
  • You can decline the SMS permission — all other features continue to work normally
  • The SMS permission is never used for any other purpose
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate Family Manager:

  • To sync financial and family data between your paired devices in real time
  • To calculate your monthly dashboard, cashflow projection, and loan schedules
  • To send push notifications for occasion reminders and bill due dates you have opted into
  • To improve stability through anonymised Firebase crash reports

We never: sell your data · share with advertisers · use your financial content for AI training

Section 04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) in the asia-south1 region (Mumbai, India). Firebase is certified under ISO 27001, SOC 1/2/3, and GDPR.

  • Authentication is handled by Google Sign-In — your password is managed by Google, not us
  • Biometric lock (if enabled) is processed entirely on-device using Android's BiometricManager
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict each family's data to authenticated members of that family only

Section 06

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at nalkudilstudio@gmail.com. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Section 07

Children's Data

Family Manager is designed for adults managing a household. Children's names and school or appointment information entered by parents is:

  • Accessible only to the parents of that family
  • Never shared or sold to any third party
  • Deleted along with the parent's account upon request
Section 08
