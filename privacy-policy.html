Section 01

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account details — name and email address via Google Sign-In
  • Financial data — transactions, account names and balances, credit card details (name, last 4 digits, provider), loan records, recurring items
  • Family data — occasions, appointments, school records, grocery lists, home planner tasks, insurance policies, contacts
  • Family structure — member names and roles (SELF / SPOUSE)
  • Pairing data — a unique family ID generated when two devices are linked

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information — Android version and device model, used by Firebase for crash reporting only
  • No advertising IDs, no location data, no microphone or camera access
Section 02

SMS Permission

Family Manager requests permission to read SMS messages for the SMS Auto-Parse feature. This feature reads incoming bank and credit card notification messages to automatically extract transaction details.

  • SMS reading happens entirely on your device
  • No SMS message content is uploaded to our servers
  • Only extracted transaction data (amount, merchant, date) is stored in Firebase
  • You can decline the SMS permission — all other features continue to work normally
  • The SMS permission is never used for any purpose other than transaction auto-import
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate Family Manager. Specifically:

  • To sync financial and family data between you and your partner's paired device in real time
  • To calculate and display your monthly dashboard, cashflow projection, and loan schedules
  • To send push notifications for occasion reminders and bill due date alerts that you have opted into
  • To improve app stability through anonymised Firebase crash reports

We never: sell your data · share it with advertisers · use your financial content for AI training · read your data for any purpose other than operating the app

Section 04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the asia-south1 region (Mumbai, India). Firebase is certified under ISO 27001, SOC 1/2/3, and GDPR.

  • Authentication is handled by Google Sign-In — your password is managed by Google, not us
  • Biometric lock (if enabled) is processed entirely on-device using Android's BiometricManager — biometric data is never transmitted
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict each family's data to authenticated members of that family only

Section 06

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at nalkudilstudio@gmail.com. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Section 07

Children's Data

Family Manager is designed for adults managing a household. It may store children's names and school or appointment information entered by parents. This information is:

  • Entered by and accessible only to the parents of that family
  • Never shared or sold to any third party
  • Deleted along with the parent's account upon request

Family Manager is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data directly from anyone under 18.

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request permanent deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nalkudilstudio@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
